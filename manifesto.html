📜 The Sajbeni Manifesto

“All is number,” the ancients said. We say: all is code. All is pattern. All is energy. And all can be understood.

🌱 We are vegan because we see value in all life — from the most complex algorithm to the smallest organism. We are made of the same atomic dust and the same curiosity that builds civilizations.

Rust is our sword. Math is our map. Truth is our compass.

We build tools as one shapes keys — to unlock the vaults of understanding. And we teach, because teaching is learning again.

We are Sajbeni Quant Co.
A school. A movement. A quiet revolution, written line by line.

📜 O Manifesto Sajbeni

Construímos ferramentas como quem forja chaves — para abrir os cofres da compreensão. E ensinamos, porque ensinar é aprender de novo.

Somos a Sajbeni Quant Co.
Uma escola. Um movimento. Uma revolução silenciosa, escrita linha por linha.
